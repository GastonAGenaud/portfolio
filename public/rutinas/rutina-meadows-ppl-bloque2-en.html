How to read this sheet
  • ✚ = NEW exercise this block · ★ = Meadows overlay · 🔁 = swapped.
  • QR = scan to watch the technique video (YouTube search).
  • S1–S3 = logged sets weight×reps@RIR. S4 sug. (gray) = next week's progression.
  • RIR = reps in reserve. 1-2 RIR on isolations, 1-3 on compounds. No need for failure on everything — 1-2 RIR captures ~90-95% of the growth with much less fatigue.
  • LP = lengthened partials: once you hit failure in the full ROM, keep going with partials in the bottom/stretched portion.
