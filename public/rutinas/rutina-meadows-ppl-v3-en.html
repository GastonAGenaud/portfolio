How to read this sheet

⬜ Saturday — Active rest

Pick ONE option (don't combine):
  • Swimming — 45 min, relaxed pace.
  • Long walk — 8,000-10,000 steps outdoors.
  • Outdoor cycling — 45 min, zone 1-2 (110-125 bpm).

Rule: no weights, no HIIT, no trying to "make the most" of the day.

  • No mandatory gym. Normal walking is fine, without pushing the step counter.
